// Help & info

Features

Per verse: compare translations, copy text (with or without reference), link to original text / interlinear (biblehub.com)
Per verse: link to Bible commentary (bibleref.com) — note: not all OT verses have commentary yet
Search: stats panel with data overview and distribution of hits across the Bible
Share the URL to share your current view (passage, search, version)
Install as app: add to home screen (iOS: Share → Add to Home Screen, Android: browser menu → Install) for a full-screen app experience

Text search operators (can be combined)

"tro"All verses containing exact word
"evig liv"Exact phrase

nåde OR frelseEither word (OR must be uppercase)
nåde frelseBoth words (AND, implicit)
